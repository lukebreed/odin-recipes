Japanese Curry with Panko Crusted Chicken

  • One yellow onion
  • 5 medium sized potatos
  • 5 carrots
  • 32 ounces of chicken broth
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 6 ounces of Golden Curry roux cubes
  • 4 ounces of all-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika

For the curry sauce:

  1. Dice onion, potatos, and carrots in varying sizes.
  2. Heat a large pot or dutch oven on medium to medium high heat.
  3. Add a drizzle of olive oil followed by diced vegetables.
  4. Once vegetable are softened, fill up with chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  5. When broth is boiling, add in roux cubes.

For the fried chicken cutlets:

  1. Slice chicken in half making two cutlets.
  2. Pound cutlets into an even thickness.
  3. Season flour with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Whisk 2 eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Coat chicken cutlets in flour, followed by the egg wash, and then the breadcrumbs.
  6. Fry until golden brown.
