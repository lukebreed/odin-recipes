Mozzerella sticks for beginners.

A recipe by Luis.

  • String cheese
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Bread crumbs
  1. Dip the string cheese into the flour, then the eggs, and finally the bread crumbs.
  2. Fill a pot a quarter of the way with vegetable oil.
  3. Put the string cheese into the oil and deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Put cheese of your choice on top of the mozzerella sticks.
  5. Finally, use a blow torch to melt the cheese of your choice.

If you would like, you could add pepperoni and/or salami to the cheese sticks.

If you would like, add hot sauce and/or spicy seasonings of your choice to add a little kick to it.

That's how you make mozzerella sticks for beginners.
